## Product Description Structure (SEO Formula)

### Template: 350+ Words

```
[TITLE - 60 chars with keyword]
Premium [Color/Style] [Category] - [Unique Benefit]

---

[INTRO - 100-150 words]

Opening paragraph must:
✓ Include primary keyword naturally
✓ State main benefit in first sentence
✓ Address customer pain point
✓ Create urgency/desire

Example:
"Introducing our Premium Black Hoodie - the perfect blend of luxury comfort 
and modern style. Made from 100% premium cotton, this designer hoodie offers 
the softness your skin loves and the durability that lasts. Whether you're 
looking for a versatile casual piece or a statement luxury item, this 
high-quality hoodie delivers. Perfect for fashion-conscious shoppers who 
refuse to compromise on quality or style."

Keywords: premium, black, hoodie, luxury, casual, high-quality, designer

---

[WHY CHOOSE THIS PRODUCT - 100 words]

✓ Unique selling points
✓ Include secondary keywords
✓ Mention benefits not features

Example:
"Why Our Premium Hoodies Stand Out:
✓ Premium 100% cotton feels incredibly soft against skin
✓ Luxury construction withstands 100+ washes without fading
✓ Contemporary design flatters all body types
✓ Premium dyeing process ensures color vibrancy
✓ Eco-conscious manufacturing with sustainable practices"

Keywords: premium, cotton, luxury, sustainable, eco-conscious

[SPECIFICATIONS - 50-75 words]

Material: 100% Premium Cotton

✓ Use exact keyword variations
```

## SEO Writing Tips

### Use Active Voice
❌ "This hoodie was made from premium cotton"
✅ "We crafted this premium hoodie from 100% cotton"

### Use Power Words (2-3 per section)
- Premium, Luxury, Quality, Durable, Exceptional
- Perfect, Ultimate, Best, Superior, Exclusive
- Comfortable, Soft, Breathable, Elegant, Timeless

### Create Urgency
✅ "Limited quantity available"
✅ "This style is trending"
✅ "Only 5 left in stock"

### Address Objections
❌ Don't mention: Price too high, sizing issues, quality concerns
✅ Do mention: "Luxury comes at price, but worth every dollar"
✅ Do mention: "Free size exchange if it doesn't fit perfectly"
✅ Do mention: "Lasts 2+ years with proper care"

### Use Emotional Language
❌ "This is a black hoodie"
✅ "Feel the luxury of premium cotton. This black hoodie hugs your skin with softness, 
making you feel confident and comfortable all day"
